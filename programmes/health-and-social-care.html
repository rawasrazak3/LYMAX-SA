Who is this course for

Current Health and Social Care Workers

This qualification is ideal for those seeking career progression, with over 1.5 million people employed in the UK care sector (Skills for Care, 2023). It equips learners with advanced management skills to take on supervisory or managerial roles.

Aspiring Managers

This course provides the knowledge and competencies required to manage teams effectively, addressing the growing demand for skilled managers in the UK's £46.2 billion social care market (NHS Digital, 2023).

Career Changers

With the UK care sector facing a workforce shortage of over 165,000 positions (Skills for Care, 2023), this qualification offers a pathway for career changers to enter a rewarding and high-demand field.

International Students

This course is internationally recognised, making it a valuable credential for those aiming to work in diverse health and social care systems worldwide.
