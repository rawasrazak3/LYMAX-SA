Job Market Demand for Accounting and Finance Roles

Financial Analyst

Analyse financial data to support decision-making, with strong demand in the UK job market. Average salary: £40,000 - £60,000.

Management Accountant

Prepare financial reports for internal use, ensuring compliance with regulations. Average salary: £35,000 - £55,000.

Audit Associate

Examine financial records to ensure accuracy and compliance. High demand for audit skills in the UK. Average salary: £30,000 - £50,000.

Tax Advisor

Provide expert advice on tax planning and compliance. Growing demand for tax professionals. Average salary: £45,000 - £65,000.
