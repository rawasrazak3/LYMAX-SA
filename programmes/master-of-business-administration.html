Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Advanced Entry Route

Starts in April, July, October and January

Apply Now

Accelerate your path to becoming an impactful leader with our MBA Advanced Entry route

An MBA is an important breakthrough in the development of senior managers or aspiring directors, as it brings together the fundamental and real-world skills, knowledge, and attributes of what it takes to lead people and deliver results.

An MBA is usually studied once you've gained a few years of professional experience. Achievement of this degree can lead to greater professional prospects, improved professional reputation and a wider business network.

The programme exposes you to a global network, diverse student group, career opportunities, and rigorous academic experience.

The MBA Advanced Entry route programme is offered to students that already hold relevant practical hands-on management experience and a recognised Postgraduate Diploma (PgD) in management or an equivalent qualification.

Programme Information and Delivery

The Advanced Entry route programme consists of a Research Methods module and a Consultancy Project Report on a related topic of your choice, making it a popular option for individuals committed to developing their careers in senior management.

Research Methods – including Research Proposal

The programme begins with Induction and Research Methods webinars, which enable you to finalise your proposal for your project.

Consultancy Project Report

The Consultancy Project Report module provides the opportunity for students to examine an area of particular interest within an appropriate organisational context. The module is designed to make a major contribution to professional and intellectual development, through enabling students to demonstrate their capacity for sustained independent thought, learning and critical reflection.

The purpose of this programme is to deliver a practical and realistic solution to a business-related strategic challenge, which in turn is supported by appropriate reference to theoretical and conceptual analysis. Students will evidence this by producing a Consultancy Project Report.

The Consultancy Project Report is delivered and assessed by our UK based academic team using our virtual learning platform and direct support from your allocated supervisor.

The MBA Advanced Entry route takes approximately 6 - 9 months to complete working with the support of your dedicated supervisor for the final Consultancy Project Report module.

Support

We want students on our course to have the support they need to make the most of their investment in their own future.

You will receive excellent support from our academic team, including your programme manager and your supervisor who will be with you every step of the way.

Entry requirements, Fees and Intakes

Students wishing to join the course must provide the following supporting documents:

 

  • A relevant Level 7 Postgraduate Diploma such as OTHM Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership (students need to submit both certificate and transcript on application)
  • A Bachelor’s degree or equivalent or higher qualification
  • A copy of your valid photo ID
  • A Statement of Purpose
  • An updated Curriculum Vitae (minimum of two years' work experience at graduate level in either a managerial or professional capacity; list job responsibilities for each role; two references - academic and professional)

We recognise the following evidence of English language proficiency.

  • A level pass in English.
  • Award of Bachelor’s degree which was studied and assessed in English
  • Kaplan Test of English (KTE) (Online test) with an overall score of 478-509 with at least 444-477 in writing and 410-443 in all other components. Click here for a discount code.
  • IELTS with an overall score of 6.5 with at least 6.0 in writing and 5.5 in all other components.
  • TOEFL with a score of 72 overall and a minimum of 18 in reading, 17 in listening, 20 in speaking and 17 in writing (including Home Edition)

In addition to the above requirements, all applicants will be required to have a live virtual interview with a staff member of London Graduate School.
Details of these arrangements will be sent subject to your successful application.
