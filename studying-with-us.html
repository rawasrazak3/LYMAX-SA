Studying with Us

Advanced Entry-degree programmes at London Graduate School are taught through student-centred activities that support the development of subject knowledge and understanding, independent learning and digital fluency.

Our online-based teaching is facilitated in a practical and collaborative manner, clearly linked to learning activity in the wider context. Opportunities are provided for students to develop autonomy, changemaker attributes and employability skills.

In addition, our approach to blended learning is a teaching and learning style that combines sense-making activities with focused interactions (with content, peers and tutors) in appropriate learning settings engaging students in knowledge construction, reflection and critique, on the development of learner autonomy and of course, on the achievement of learning outcomes.

At London Graduate School we are proud to offer study which provides

  • Flexibility in learning
  • Efficiency to make the best use of your time
  • Effective learning as you can apply your learning to your own experience and vice versa
  • Personal approach provided by the whole programme team
  • Diverse learning styles using a range of teaching and learning approaches

We enable your learning to happen 24 hours a day to suit you and to utilise a wide range of teaching and learning resources with our academic staff and using digital facilities as well as tutorials and other means.

Most of our students work alongside their studies in addition to having family commitments too. So, our approach enables you to study when and where it suits you around your existing life and commitments.

Support

  • Learning with London Graduate School means that wherever you are, you’ll get regular academic support from our teaching staff who will guide you through your modules and offer advice and feedback every step of the way.
  • You will be allocated a personal tutor who can give additional guidance and help to keep you on track.
  • You will also get to know the Programme team who are on hand for any administrative or technical questions at any time.
  • And don’t forget, you’ll be interacting with your fellow students through our forums and discussions, giving you access to a wealth of alternative perspectives, approaches, and peer support.
