About Us

London Graduate School is an established and dynamic institution that offers Advanced Entry degree programmes designed for students to gain knowledge and skills for a fast-changing and complex world.

Working with our university partners, we strive to deliver high-quality learning environments to meet the needs of the modern world.

Our Vision

As a global education institution, diversity is our strength. Our vision is based on cultivating and sharing knowledge in an international environment for today’s global citizens by widening opportunities for those who wish to engage in higher education and learning and also those who might otherwise be unable to do so due to access of opportunity and other reasons.

Our Approach

We promote learning and sharing of knowledge in a global context by offering a blend of teaching and learning methods that combine personal and professional development with world-class academic knowledge, global leadership and vision.

The transformative potential of education and learning and the enhancements it brings to the economic and personal wellbeing of students and teaching staff and for society as a whole, is at the heart what we do.

In all that we do, we aim to be outstanding, responsive and collaborative.

Sustainability

We have embedded sustainable development through our learning, teaching and culture and to inspire individuals to develop in their professions to make a difference in a global society.

Supporting the vision of widening global access to higher education, we believe in contributing to the UNESCO Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) and the global education and higher education target to “ensure equal access for all women and men to affordable quality technical, vocational and tertiary education, including university.” (UNESCO 2015)
